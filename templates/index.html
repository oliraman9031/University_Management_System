About UMS

The University Management System (UMS) is a robust, web-based platform designed to simplify and automate the complex administrative and academic operations within a university. It ensures data integrity, enhances communication, and provides easy access to essential information for everyone involved.

Key Features

Student Portal

Manage profiles, enroll in courses, view results, check schedules, and pay fees online.

Faculty Dashboard

Oversee assigned courses, manage exams, evaluate results, and view student lists.

Admin Control Panel

Comprehensive control over students, faculty, courses, departments, fees, and system settings.

Course Management

Add, update, or remove courses, assign faculty, and manage course details and credits.

Exam & Results

Schedule exams, manage evaluation statuses (Unevaluated, Evaluated, Locked), and publish results securely.

Fee Management

Automated fee generation, online payment tracking, and status updates (Pending/Paid).
